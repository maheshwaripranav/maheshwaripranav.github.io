About me

I'm a Quantum Computing and Information researcher with background in Physics. I enjoy working on quantum algorithms, error correction techniques and ever challenging quantum hardware implementations. I am also interested in quantum networks and quantum communication.

I write codes to implement error correction techniques, my blog also features the theory to understand what happens at the background. I also have experience with quantum machine learning and loves to work on open machine learning algorithms. Do check out my past projects on support vector machines and neural networks.
